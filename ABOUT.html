About the cat Karma

Some info about Karma

Karma is a really fluffy cat.
 Karma was born on the 27th of April in the year 2017.
 Karma has given birth to 14 kittens.
 She has won many medals in cat beauty contests.
 Karma really loves to lick plastic and play with gluey stuff.
 She also loves when you give her candy and pet her on the head or scratch her neck.
 When you open any drawer in the kitchen she thinks shes about to get candy and runs to the kitchen as fast as she can.
 We bought her on the 14th of December in the year 2020.
 Karma is loved by everyone she meets and is very lovely towards everyone.
